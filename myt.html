MYTILUS 1943-1953

copyright - WHOI		 Built: 1939
Length: 41'
Beam: 10'
Draft: 5'

In 1943 the Woods Hole Oceanographic Institution purchased Mytilus from the Coastal and Island Waterways, which operated between Cape Ann and Sandy Hook, New Jersey.  WHOI used Mytilus, named for a blue mussel, for local runs and meteorology.  The vessel was sold in 1953.
